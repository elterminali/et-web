Introduction

Getting Started

Welcome to eltrm! Follow these steps to get up and running quickly.

  • 1

    Create Your Account

    Download the app or visit app.eltrm.com and sign up with your email address.

  • 2

    Set Up Your First Warehouse

    Create your first warehouse location to start tracking inventory.

  • 3

    Add Your Products

    Add products manually, scan barcodes, or import from Excel/CSV to build your inventory quickly.

  • 4

    Start Managing

    Track stock movements, set alerts, and generate reports.

Feature

Barcode Scanning

Turn your phone into a powerful barcode scanner.

Supported Formats

1D Barcodes: EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, UPC-E, Code 128, Code 39, ITF
2D Barcodes: QR Code, Data Matrix, PDF417

Scanning Tips

  • Ensure good lighting conditions
  • Hold phone 6-8 inches from the barcode
  • Keep the entire barcode visible in the viewfinder
  • Clean your camera lens for best results
  • Use continuous scan mode for bulk operations

Feature

Bulk Stock Import

Import multiple stocks at once using Excel or CSV files.

How to Import Stocks

  • 1

    Download Template

    Download a sample Excel or CSV template from the Import Stocks page.

  • 2

    Fill in Your Data

    Add your stock information following the template format. Barcode is required, other fields are optional.

  • 3

    Upload File

    Select your filled Excel or CSV file and upload it. The system will validate and preview your data.

  • 4

    Review Preview

    Check the preview of your stocks before importing. Fix any errors shown.

  • 5

    Confirm Import

    Click Import to add all valid stocks to your inventory. You'll see a summary of successful and failed imports.

Import File Fields

Required: Barcode
Optional: Stock Name, Stock Code, Quantity, Purchase Price, Sale Price, VAT, Notification Limit

Feature

Stock Alerts

Never run out of stock with automated low stock alerts.

How It Works

Set minimum stock thresholds for each product. When inventory falls below the threshold, you'll receive notifications via email, push notifications, and in-app alerts.

Collaboration

Team Management

Invite team members and manage access permissions.

User Roles

  • Admin: Full access to all features and settings
  • Manager: Can manage inventory and view reports
  • Staff: Can perform stock transactions only
  • Viewer: Read-only access to inventory data
Inviting Members

Go to Settings > Team Management > Invite Member. Enter their email and select the appropriate role. They'll receive an email invitation to join.

How to Subscribe

  • Subscribe on Mobile

    Open the app, go to Settings > Subscription, and choose a plan.

  • Subscribe on Web

    Visit app.eltrm.com, go to Account Settings > Subscription.

Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled. You can manage your subscription through the app or your device's subscription settings.
