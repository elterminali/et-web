FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan barcodes with my phone?
Open the app and tap the barcode scanner icon. Point your phone's camera at the barcode and it will automatically scan. Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app in your device settings.
Can I manage multiple warehouses?
Yes! El Terminali supports multiple warehouse management. You can create different warehouse locations, track inventory separately for each, and transfer stock between them.
How do low stock alerts work?
Set minimum stock thresholds for each product. When inventory falls below the threshold, you'll receive automatic notifications via email and in-app alerts to help you reorder in time.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transmission. Your data is stored on secure servers with regular backups. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.
Can I export my inventory data?
Yes, you can export your inventory data in CSV and Excel formats. This feature is available in the Reports section of both the web dashboard and mobile app.
How do I add team members?
Go to Settings > Team Management in the web dashboard. Invite team members by email and assign different permission levels (Admin, Manager, Staff) to control their access.
