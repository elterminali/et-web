Handdy respects your privacy. This privacy policy is effective as of April 1, 2016 and explains how we collect and use the information we receive from you and will assist you in making decisions about using our services. It applies to all platforms (iOS, Android, and web) on which the app is used.

This privacy policy covers the following topics:
• What information we collect from you,
• How we use the information we collect from you,
• What measures we take to protect the information we collect from you,
• What happens to the information we collect and control in case of loss or unauthorized use.

Information Collection
Handdy may collect information from you through various methods. Server logs are the first of these methods. Server logs are used to collect information about browser type, ISP, IP address, which site you came from, which site you went to, which service you used and on which platform you used that service, the number of page views and clicks from all of our users. Our site also uses standard technology called ‘cookies’. Information such as viewed pages and time spent on the site can be collected through cookies. This type of information is not directly associated with any user, and is used for statistical information about the use of the site and/or applications. Handdy reserves the right to associate server logs with personal information. The second method of information collection is the personal information we receive from you during registration for our service. During registration, we collect information such as name, surname, e-mail address, and password. These types of information are safely stored by us and are not shared with anyone else without your permission. Finally, as you use our site or applications, usage data will be created and collected by us. This data includes information such as the pages you visit, the time you spend on the site, and the services you use. This data is collected for the purpose of providing you with more personalized and efficient service.

Communication
We may contact you using your personal information to inform you about our services or to solve any problems you may have. There is no systematic process that you can perform to terminate this communication. However, you can inform us by contacting us that you no longer wish to be contacted in this manner.

marketing services
Occasionally, we may send you emails that contain special offers or information about our other services. If you do not wish to receive such marketing or informational emails, you can unsubscribe from our email list at any time by using the 'Unsubscribe' link found at the bottom of the emails we send you.

Security
Handdy takes all necessary precautions as prescribed by industry standards to ensure the security of your information. The information you provide during registration or while using the site/app is transmitted using a secure security system (Secure Socket Layer or SSL). Access to such information is restricted within our company as well, only employees who have a specific need to perform their duties are given access. Additionally, all of our employees are regularly informed about our privacy policies. The servers where your information is stored are kept in locked environments in closed places accessible to only employees. If there is a security breach regarding your information, Handdy will inform you via e-mail in accordance with the laws. Therefore, it is your responsibility and important that the communication information you provide us is correct and up-to-date. If your information is incorrect or incomplete, you can update your information on our site or apps. You can also update your information by sending an email to info@eltrm.com. Updating requests sent by email may take up to 10 business days.

Updates to Privacy Policies
If there is a need for an update on our privacy policy regarding what information is collected, how it is used and shared, the changes will be notified to you from here and other appropriate places.
